How Agent Guardrail Lab works

What you are simulating

This app is a tiny, opinionated model of how an AI agent can cause a production incident when autonomy, access, and ambiguity combine. It is not a real risk assessment.

Try this experiment

Turn OFF approvals and backups, raise autonomy, and choose "Ops pressure". Run a few times with the same seed and notice how outcomes differ when observability is low.

Guardrails modeled

Human approvals, read-only defaults, dry-run, verified backups, and sandboxing each reduce the probability of irreversible actions or improve rollback.
